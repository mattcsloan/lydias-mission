2017: A Lydia’s Mission Year in Review January 01, 2018

God has been moving in powerful ways this past year.

Lydia’s Mission has expanded in ways I could never have dreamed. Hope has been restored, the Gospel has been preached and many women have come into relationships with Christ. God has been so faithful.

As a Lydia’s Mission partner, your monthly and one-time financial support and prayers helps to secure regular income for South African women eager to feed, educate and provide for their families:

  • The micro-business, Lydia’s Market Sewing Project, expanded significantly
  • 9 fulltime Sewing women hired
  • Made over 1000 stuffed animals with 5 products
  • Sold in over 7 countries
  • Purchased 6 sewing machines
  • 5 Lydia’s Circle women’s groups meeting weekly this past year, with about 100 women each week
  • 3 of these groups met for the first time:
    • Lydia’s Circle: Seniors
    • Lydia’s Circle: Farm Village
    • Lydia’s Circle: 2nd Generation

I’m so thankful for all the support, encouragement and kind words. Lydia’s Mission would not be where it is today without your support. Thank you for partnering with God to make a difference one life at a time!

God is continuing to move with a large expansion in the first quarter of 2018. Can’t wait to see what He is going to do this coming year!

Happy New Year!

Claire Brown
